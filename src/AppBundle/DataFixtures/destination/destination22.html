Croisière kitesurf aux îles Cayman

Les croisières kite aux îles Cayman nous permettent de combiner toutes nos activités de predilection: kitesurf, surf, plongée, ainsi qu'animation nocturne!

Kitesurfing cruise to the Cayman islands

Grand Cayman, Cayman Brac and Little Cayman lie on the turquoise waters of the western Caribbean and offer kiteboarders and watermen a dream location.
Blessed with sun-kissed beaches and waters teeming with gold flecked fish, the Cayman Islands offers some of the best diving and snorkelling in the world.

Kitesurfing cruises to the Cayman allow us to combine all of our favourites activties: kiteboarding, surfing, scuba diving, snorkeling, and an active nightlife!

Please note that cruises to the Cayman Islands are solely custom made: if you'd like to organise a cruise there, please fill our custom made cruise enquiry form.
