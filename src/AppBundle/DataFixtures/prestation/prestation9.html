Ah tiens... il semblerait que nous ayons un peu de retard sur la traduction de cette page: on s'affole, désolés pour le désagrément.

Scuba diving is a very attractive and fun activity, regularly practised by most of our skippers and by numerous amount of our kitesurfing guests.
Its is for many on the top 3 of the most wanted acticities along a caribbean cruise.

Offering a great alternative to no wind days, diving is a great opportunity to visit the underwater world.

If all our skippers have fins and snorkels on board, not all of them offer the diving option.
The ones who do offer either the facility on board (compressor and tanks), either the easy access to the activity via local diving schools with which they have special price arrangements.

The diving cost is never included in the price and always offered on option: the price, if known, will be given in the cruise detail. If not clearly stated, or if you'd like additional information, contact the skipper of the cruise (simply click on the "enquiry" button).

Kite cruises offering diving are usually dedicated to experienced riders.

Important:
Note that diving is offered on all of our destinations and a dive can be included to almost any kitesurfing cruise: if you'd like to do a -single- dive along a kite cruise, there's no need to select "diving" as criteria. All our skippers will be more than happy to set you up with a diving school along your kiting trip. One shall select "diving" as cruise type solely if willing to involve numerous amount of dives along with the kitesurfing itinerary.

Who's a kite and dive cruise for?

Ideal for groups of kitesurfers already keen divers or willing to get certified, as well as groups of kitesurfers including non riders keen scuba divers.

Finding a boat charter offering kitesurf and diving

If youd' like to do a kite and diving cruise, select the option in the search engine.

If you would like more info on a cruise featuring the option, simply click on « enquiry » to ask any question you may have to the skipper of the cruise.
